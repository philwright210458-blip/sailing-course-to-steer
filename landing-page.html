What it does

Everything a navigator needs

Built around the RYA vector triangle method — the same technique taught in Day Skipper and Coastal Skipper courses.

🗺️
Interactive Nautical Chart

Plot routes on a real nautical chart with OpenSeaMap overlays. Tap to place waypoints, drag to adjust, and see your passage come together visually.

🧭
Course to Steer

Accurate CTS calculated using the velocity triangle — accounting for tidal stream, boat speed, and leeway. Outputs true or magnetic bearing with your local variation applied.

🌊
Tidal Atlas

Visualise tidal streams along your route for each hour of the passage. Multi-hour legs use proper vector summation so opposing tides cancel correctly.

💨
Live Wind Overlays

See real-time wind barbs on the chart. Set local wind direction per leg for accurate leeway correction on each segment of your passage.

📍
Waypoint Coordinates

Every waypoint shown in nautical DDM format — degrees and decimal minutes — ready to punch into your chart plotter or log book.

A clean, focused tool with a simple annual subscription. No banner ads, no in-app purchases, no distractions. Just the passage planning tools you need.
